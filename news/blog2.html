# Top 5 Mods for Farming Simulator 25 **May 5, 2025** Hello everyone! Recently, I released a video titled ["Farming Simulator 25: Top 5 Mods You Need to Try Right Now!"](https://youtu.be/TNKpz2OZt0k). In this blog post, I will be rating the top 5 mods. --- ## 1. Unlimited Color Configuration *By GIANTS Software* This mod allows you to set custom colors on almost every vehicle. It's very cool if you want to your vehicles to be colorful. **Rating: 8/10** ![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/FarmCraft9/farmcraft-software/refs/heads/main/assets/images/UnlimiedColorConfiguration1.jpg) --- ## 2. Liftable Pallets and Bales *By Jos* This is an mod that allows you to lift pallets and bales with hand that are over 200kg. It's useful if you are working with pallets. **Rating: 7/10** ![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/FarmCraft9/farmcraft-software/refs/heads/main/assets/images/LiftablePalletsandBales1.jpg) --- ## 3. John Deere XUV865M *By GIANTS Software* This is my favorite mod, this mod adds an John Deere car to Farming Simulator 25 and It's nice for forestry and transport. **Rating: 9/10** ![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/FarmCraft9/farmcraft-software/refs/heads/main/assets/images/JohnDeereXUV865M1.jpg) --- ## 4. Full Refund *By vonNiklasson* This is an mod that allows you to sell buildings for that same price as bought. **Rating: 8/10** ![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/FarmCraft9/farmcraft-software/refs/heads/main/assets/images/FullRefund1.jpg) --- ## 5. Production Point Leasing *By olaf.tnt* This mod allows you to lease production buildings like vehicles. **Rating: 7/10** ![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/FarmCraft9/farmcraft-software/refs/heads/main/assets/images/ProductionPointLeasing1.jpg) ### Thanks for Reading!
