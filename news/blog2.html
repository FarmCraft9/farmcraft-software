Top 5 Mods for Farming Simulator 25

May 5, 2025

Hello everyone! Recently, I released a video titled "Farming Simulator 25: Top 5 Mods You Need to Try Right Now!". In this blog post, I will be rating the top 5 mods.

1. Unlimited Color Configuration

By GIANTS Software

This mod allows you to set custom colors on almost every vehicle. It's very cool if you want your vehicles to be colorful.

Rating: 8/10

2. Liftable Pallets and Bales

By Jos

This is a mod that allows you to lift pallets and bales with hand that are over 200kg. It's useful if you are working with pallets.

Rating: 7/10

3. John Deere XUV865M

By GIANTS Software

This is my favorite mod, this mod adds a John Deere car to Farming Simulator 25 and it's nice for forestry and transport.

Rating: 9/10

4. Full Refund

By vonNiklasson

This is a mod that allows you to sell buildings for the same price as bought.

Rating: 8/10

5. Production Point Leasing

By olaf.tnt

This mod allows you to lease production buildings like vehicles.

Rating: 7/10

Thanks for Reading!
